HOW TO PLAY:

Player One uses W to go UP and S to go DOWN.

Player Two uses the UP Arrow to go up and the Down Arrow to go DOWN.

While in the game, you can return to the main menu by selecting backspace.

To exit game, you'll need to do so from the menu.

HOORAY!

Tips for winning from the pros:

MOVE FIRST! -- the other player cannot move while you are moving.
BE READY TO MOVE! -- If stuck not being able to move, wait until your opponant pauses or changes directions - THAT IS YOUR TIME TO STRIKE!
